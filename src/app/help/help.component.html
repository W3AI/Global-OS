The Game of Web Life

Everyone can play the Game of Web Life.

The objective is to build during your web career
as many useful web genes as possible.

Top winners will have their names written accross the web clouds and their web genes will serve generations to come.

Just one simple rule: Try to help as many players as possible in exchange for web recognition and real money.

Your AI Unicorn will help you along the way, matching challenges to the skills in your social network.

Here's how to play:

1. Copy your AI on your Google Cloud account.

2. List your interests on the setup page.

3. Create projects and skills using the AI forms.

4. List your friends on the links page.

5. Run your AI to match projects to skills.

6. Adjust your terms to maximize the deals.
